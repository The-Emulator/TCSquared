God is One in Three Persons (The Trinity)

God the Father – Creator and sustainer of the universe. God the Son (Jesus Christ) – God incarnate, Savior of the world. God the Holy Spirit – God’s presence and power within believers. “Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit.” – Matthew 28:19

God is Love

Central to God's nature is love. God’s love was fully revealed through the life, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ. “God is love. Whoever lives in love lives in God, and God in them.” – 1 John 4:16 God is Holy and Just God is completely pure and morally perfect. He judges sin but also provides mercy and forgiveness through Christ. “Holy, holy, holy is the Lord Almighty; the whole earth is full of His glory.” – Isaiah 6:3

God is the Creator

God created the heavens, the earth, and everything in them. He made humanity in His own image. “In the beginning God created the heavens and the earth.” – Genesis 1:1 God is Faithful and Unchanging God’s character never changes; He is constant and reliable His promises endure forever. “Jesus Christ is the same yesterday and today and forever.” – Hebrews 13:8

God Desires a Relationship with People

Christianity teaches that God wants a personal relationship with each person. This relationship is restored through faith in Jesus. “Here I am! I stand at the door and knock.” – Revelation 3:20 God is Sovereign God has supreme power and authority over all creation. Nothing happens without His knowledge or permission. “Our God is in the heavens; He does all that He pleases.” – Psalm 115:3
